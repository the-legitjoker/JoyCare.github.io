Symptoms of anxiety and stress


  • Worry and apprehension

  • Restlessness

  • Sleep problems

  • Difficulty concentrating

  • Irritability

  • Sadness

  • Feeling pressure and hurried

Physical symptoms of anxiety


  • sweaty hands

  • Dry mouth

  • Muscle tension.

  • Shortness of breath.

  • Changes in heart rate

  • Headaches

Mental symptoms of anxiety


  • Nightmares.

  • Uncontrollable, obsessive thoughts

  • Flashbacks of traumatic experiences

  • Cripling fear and panic

Behavioral symptoms of anxiety


  • Inability to remain calm

  • Trouble sleeping

  • Ritualistic behaviors, such as washing hands repeatedly
