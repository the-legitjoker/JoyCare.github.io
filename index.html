MENTAL HEALTH ISSUES

Mental health is a state of emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It has an effect on the way we think, feel, and act. It also influences how we deal with tension, interact with others, and make decisions. Mental health is critical at all stages of life, including childhood, adolescence, and adulthood.

Anxiety & Stress

An occurrence that causes one to feel irritated or anxious will cause the sensation of stress. Anxiety is a sense of fear, panic, and discomfort. It can be a stress response. It can mean a more severe problem if stress and anxiety interfere with your everyday life. It may be time to request assistance.

Depression

Depression is a common and serious illness that negatively affects the way a person feels, thinks, and acts hence, affecting a person’s motivation, feelings, behaviour, and thoughts.

Disorders
